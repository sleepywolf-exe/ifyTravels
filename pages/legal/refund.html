Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 30 days or more prior to the departure date will receive a 100% refund, minus a $50 processing fee.

Cancellations made between 15 and 29 days prior to departure will receive a 50% refund.

Cancellations made within 14 days of departure are non-refundable.

2. Refund Process

Refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after cancellation approval.

3. Flight Tickets

Flight tickets booked through ifyTravels are subject to the specific airline's cancellation policy and may be non-refundable.

4. Force Majeure

In the event of cancellation due to force majeure (natural disasters, pandemics, etc.), ifyTravels will work with suppliers to secure refunds or credits where possible, but cannot guarantee full reimbursement.
